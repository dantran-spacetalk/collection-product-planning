What the app does

Spaces - organise your world
Create separate Spaces for different groups in your life - family, carers, grandparents. Each Space has its own members, settings and communication channels.
Live location, places & routes
Track your child's live location in real time. Set places like home and school, create custom safe routes, and get instant alerts when they arrive or leave.
SOS & emergency alerts
When your child hits the SOS button, every member of their Space is notified instantly with their live location. Help is never more than one press away.
Group messaging & check-ins
Secure group chats within each Space. Send check-in requests to the watch and get a quick reply - no phone calls needed.
School mode & contact management
Silences the watch during school hours automatically. You control exactly who can reach your child - no unknown numbers ever.
Battery condition
The battery has been tested prior to sale and retains at least 80% of its original capacity. Battery capacity will naturally decrease over time with normal use.
Water resistance
This watch was originally manufactured with an IP67 dust and water-resistant rating (splash protection up to 1 metre for up to 30 minutes). Water resistance seals degrade over time and through normal wear and we cannot guarantee this rating is maintained. We strongly recommend removing the watch before swimming or bathing.
