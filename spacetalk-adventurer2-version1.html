It's ideal for ages 5–14. If your child is younger or you prefer a slimmer, simpler watch, the Loop is a great alternative starting from $174. It has GPS, calls and SOS, but no camera.
Everything is managed in the free Spacetalk parent app — available on iOS and Android. You approve every contact, set safe zones, enable school mode, check location history and monitor activity. Your child can only interact with people you've approved.
The Complete Pack, Plus Pack and Starter Pack bundles all include a Watch Plan. You choose the duration (30, 180 or 365 days) using the plan switcher above. The Watch Only option doesn't include a plan, so you'd activate one separately.
The Adventurer 2 is IP67 rated — protected against splashing, rain and hand washing. It's not designed for submersion or swimming, so take it off at the pool.
