Product details

The Adventurer 2 Full Kit Bundle gives your child everything they need for safe independence. Featuring 4G video and voice calling, real-time GPS tracking, a front-facing 5MP camera, and the Spacetalk app for parent management — all wrapped in a durable, splash-proof design kids love.
  • Adventurer 2 smartwatch
  • Default colour strap (fitted)
  • Magnetic charging dock
  • USB-C charging cable
  • Quick start guide
  • SIM tool

Plus your selected plan, 2 extra colour straps, charging stand, and screen protector.

A Spacetalk wearable plan is required to use calling, messaging, GPS location, and the Spacetalk app. Plans start from $13.75/mo (365-day plan). The watch cannot function as a standalone device without an active plan.
  • 1.4" AMOLED display
  • 5MP front camera
  • 4G LTE connectivity
  • GPS + GLONASS + Galileo
  • IP67 water resistance
  • Gorilla Glass 3
  • Heart rate + step tracking
  • 730mAh battery (up to 3 days)
